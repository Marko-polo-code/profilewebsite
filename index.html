More About Me


As a dynamic professional with a proven track record in talent acquisition and business development, my journey into software engineering was sparked by my interactions with tech clients and engineers in search of exceptional teams. This ignited a drive to redefine myself as a software engineer. I believe that my prior experiences, ranging from client-focussed roles to operations, sales and business development, combined with my software engineering experience, will enable me to deliver impactful and innovative digital solutions.

Today, I am looking forward to collaborating with others on open source projects and contributing to existing codebases, learning from others, and starting my own projects.

I am trilingual (French, English, German) and I have broadened my linguistic skills to encompass computer languages (Javascript, Ruby and Python, to name a few). I look forward to continuing this journey of learning and expanding my repertoire even further.

Away from the computer, you'll likely find me outdoors, often kicking a football and captaining my local team in Berlin Friedrichshain.
